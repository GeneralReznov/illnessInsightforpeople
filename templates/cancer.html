About Voice Biomarker Analysis

This advanced cancer detection system analyzes 22 voice biomarkers including frequency variations, jitter, shimmer, and other vocal characteristics that may indicate the presence of cancer-related changes in the vocal tract.

Voice Parameters:
  • Fundamental frequency (Fo, Fhi, Flo)
  • Jitter measurements
  • Shimmer variations
Quality Measures:
  • Harmonic-to-noise ratio (HNR)
  • Recurrence period density (RPDE)
  • Pitch period entropy (PPE)
Analysis Benefits:
  • Non-invasive screening
  • Early detection potential
  • Quick assessment
